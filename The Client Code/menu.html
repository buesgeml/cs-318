Menu

Our menu is filled with classic sweet desserts, with frequently changing flavors available for both our cakes and our cupcakes! Each treat is made from scratch each day, and our nutrition facts can be found on the packaging of each item. In order to meet the late-night demand for sweet treats, we offer a limited menu in order to limit product waste and wait time.
